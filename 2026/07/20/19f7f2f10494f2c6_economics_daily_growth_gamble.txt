A San Francisco Fed paper

Source: Federal Reserve Bank of San Francisco economists Nicolas 
Petrosky-Nadeau, Yeji Sung, and Daniel J. Wilson
тАЬWe find that hard and soft data have often pointed to different levels of 
recession risk, long before the term тАШvibecessionтАЩ became popular,тАЭ three San
