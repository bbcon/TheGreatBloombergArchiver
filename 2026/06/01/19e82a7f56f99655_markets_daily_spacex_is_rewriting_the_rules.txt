Making way for SpaceX

Elon Musk’s blockbuster SpaceX IPO is so large and economically consequential 
thatparts of the market are reorganizing around it 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/features/2026-05-31/spacex-s-ipo-led-by-elon-musk-forces-index-funds-and-retail-to-change-the-rules?cmpid=060126_marketsdaily&utm_campaign=marketsdaily&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260601&utm_content=4984>
:

 * Index providers are rewriting rules governing entry into major benchmarks 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-30/nasdaq-clears-way-for-spacex-big-ipos-to-gain-fast-index-entry?cmpid=060126_marketsdaily&utm_campaign=marketsdaily&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260601&utm_content=4984>
, changes that will affect other big offerings in the pipeline, such as those 
from OpenAI and Anthropic.
 * Passive-investing firms are modeling billions of dollars in automatic 
buying flows.
 * ETF issuers are racing to launch products tied to the listing.
 * Retail traders are pouring into speculative pre-IPO vehicles 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/features/2026-03-11/spacex-openai-potential-ipos-spur-murky-deals-to-buy-stock?cmpid=060126_marketsdaily&utm_campaign=marketsdaily&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260601&utm_content=4984>
 even for indirect exposure.
Investors are being tapped for a record $75 billion, twice as much as any 
previous listing. The rocket, satellite and AI conglomerate is targeting a 
valuation of at least $1.8 trillion.

“I can’t think of a more consequential IPO in my lifetime,” said Owen Lamont 
at Acadian Asset Management.

Buying now means endorsing Musk’s ambitions to dominate the market for AI 
computing power, trusting he’ll do the same in satellite communications, and 
taking seriously his plans to find new homes for humans on other planets.

“My main goal is to buy some of the SpaceX IPO,” said Janice Vines, a recently 
retired claims coordinator and former elementary school teacher from Ohio. 
“Elon is a visionary to say the least, and I hope to ride his coattails into my 
retirement.”

 * Arm shares rally 10% after Nvidia announced its new chip for PCs, which
