Stock market predictions for 2026: The AI boom, dollar’s decline and sticky 
inflation


Our annual compilation of outlooks for the year ahead 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/graphics/2026-investment-outlooks/> features calls 
from more than 60 institutions, including Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan Wealth 
Management and the BlackRock Investment Institute. They talk of an environment 
where the AI spend and government policies are adding fuel to growth at an 
unusual stage of the business cycle. 



They also argue inflation won’t quite be tamed as a result, and that central 
banks may not have the room to maneuver that markets currently expect. And 
while private assets will continue their ascent, the dollar will continue its 
decline.
