The White House is rewriting the rules of engagement with corporate America


From public attacks on media conglomerates to the ever-present threat of new 
tariffs, President Donald Trump has spent the past year blowing past the norms 
that have traditionally governed relations between the Oval Office and titans 
of industry.


Trump’s whims carry the weight of market forces, reshaping capital flows, 
supply chains and the very balance of power between business and government. 
For CEOs, that means adopting strategies fordealing with the most 
interventionist president 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/features/2026-ceo-playbook-trump-second-term/> in 
almost a century. That can look like calling him directly — or bringing him a 
gift. Read more in this Bloomberg Businessweek cover story.
