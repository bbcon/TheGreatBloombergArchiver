The Iran war has highlighted ChinaтАЩs efforts to bulletproof its economy by

<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/china-s-exports-surge-tops-forecasts-as-iran-war-threatens-trade?cmpid=BBD040326_CN&utm_campaign=china&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260403>

<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-27/china-s-carmakers-return-to-growth-in-europe-with-ev-sales?cmpid=BBD040326_CN&utm_campaign=china&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260403>
