**European Defense Spending**
Europe's sustained push to increase defense expenditure continues to reshape procurement patterns. Turkey's arms industry — anchored by state-controlled Aselsan, which reached a $40 billion valuation earlier this year — is increasingly integrated into European defense supply chains, even as Turkey's formal EU accession process remains stalled. The pace of Turkey-EU defense market integration is described by Bloomberg Economics as a "slower burn" relative to the more rapid US-Turkey rapprochement, but the directional trend appears established.
