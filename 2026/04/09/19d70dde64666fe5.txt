Holt, an affluent area northeast of London, has become an unlikely 
battleground forBritain’s biggest discount grocers 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-09/aldi-and-lidl-turn-on-each-other-in-race-for-uk-discount-shoppers?cmpid=040926_morningemea&utm_campaign=morningemea&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260409>
.

 * Both Aldi and Lidl, the German chains that upended the UK grocery market 
since arriving in the early 1990s, want to open in the town.
 * The two sites in Holt are just over half a mile apart, and would be a 
dramatic change for the 4,000 or so residents.
 * Aldi applied in January to open in a residential area, while Lidl applied 
last July.
 * Aldi objected to its competitor’s plans, arguing that allowing both would 
have a “significant adverse impact” on the town center.
