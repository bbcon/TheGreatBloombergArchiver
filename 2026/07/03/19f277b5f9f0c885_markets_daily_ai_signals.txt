What can investors learn from a token?

Tokens are the basic units of data in large language models like ChatGPT and 
Claude. As traders try and figure out whatтАЩs coming next in the volatile AI 
trade, theyтАЩretracking what users are paying for this basic commodity 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-03/the-ai-trade-is-losing-one-of-its-key-signals-taking-stock?cmpid=070326_marketsdaily&utm_campaign=marketsdaily&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260703&utm_content=6207>
.

The Silicon Data LLM Token Expenditure Index, which blends token price and 
usage, is one way to do that. ItтАЩs down almost 20% from a high in May after 
nearly doubling since its inception in December.

One explanation for the recent decline is that AI companies are losing pricing 
power with increasingly cost-sensitive customers, and that expectations for an 
eventual AI bonanza could prove misplaced.

Another read is that total spend has roughly doubled since last year and 
cheaper tokens have expanded the market. This means than an index pause is 
simply digestion, while demand is real and capex is money well spent. тАФ
