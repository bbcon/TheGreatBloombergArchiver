OpenAI warned US lawmakers that China’s DeepSeek has used increasingly 
sophisticated distillation techniques <https://bloom.bg/4aA9k73> to extract 
output from leading US AI models to train its R1 chatbot. The company said the 
methods were designed to evade safeguards and free-ride on American AI 
advances. Meanwhile, the CIA released aMandarin recruitment video 
<https://bloom.bg/46Is42X> targeting Chinese military officials, seeking to 
exploit discontent within the People’s Liberation Army after President Xi 
Jinping’s sweeping purges.

Asian equities slipped 1% from a record after US stocks tumbled 
<https://bloom.bg/4aho4cd> as tech profit fears hit megacaps. Gold and silver 
rebounded after sharp losses, while US Treasuries eased ahead of today's 
inflation data. Logistics stocks slumped after a former karaoke company with a 
stock-market value of$6 million  <https://bloom.bg/3O6zeYJ> touted a logistics 
platform, sparking an AI-driven selloff that spilled into other sectors.

 Deep Dive: Reverberations 



Four years on from Russia’s full-scale invasion, the economic, political and 
security fallout is reverberating far beyond Ukraine’s borders.

 * Vladimir Putin’s all-out assault on Feb. 24, 2022, has  devastated 
Ukrainian cities <https://bloom.bg/462lHHM>, claimed hundreds of thousands of 
lives and forced more than 10 million people to flee their homes—nearly 7 
million of them abroad. 
 * The loss of access to Russian natural gas led to a dramatic rise in costs 
for energy-intensive industries across Europe, from chemicals to autos and 
machinery. 
 * The conflict has raised existential questions about Europe’s ability to 
deter — or coexist with—an aggressive Russia under Putin, and about its 
readiness if he isn’t stopped in Ukraine.
