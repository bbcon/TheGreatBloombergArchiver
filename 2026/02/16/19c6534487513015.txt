content. He’s set to pledge to ensure they comply withlaws designed to protect 
children online <https://bloom.bg/3MJa168> in a speech today. Current laws only

 Deep Dive: Sweden Warms Up to the Euro 


 <https://bloom.bg/4cvFqDu> Photographer: Andrey Rudakov/Bloomberg 
After Russia’s invasion of Ukraine triggered an about-face on NATO membership, 
Sweden is starting to consideranother historic shift <https://bloom.bg/4cvFqDu>
: adopting the euro.

 * While the debate is in its early stages, a subtle but notable shift is 
taking place as conditions change. In 2003, when voters rejected Europe’s 
common currency in a referendum, the focus was on the krona’s ability to act as 
a buffer for the largest Nordic economy, but now geopolitical concerns have 
added a new dimension <https://bloom.bg/4tEs65Y>. 
 * On top of threats from Russia and China, Trump’s America First foreign 
policy—including threats to acquire Greenland from neighboring 
Denmark—underscore how smaller economies are exposed in an era of great-power 
rivalries. 
 * “Sweden is now a full member of NATO and we are reinforcing our defenses 
alongside our EU partners,” Cecilia Rönn, a Swedish lawmaker from the Liberal 
party, said in an interview. “But we are still standing with one foot outside, 
in that we are not part of the currency cooperation.”
