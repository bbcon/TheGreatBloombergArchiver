Christine Lagarde is expected to leave the European Central Bank before her 
eight-year term ends <https://bloom.bg/4rWORQW> in October 2027, the FT 
reported. The ECB president wants to exit before the French presidential 
election in April next year. The ECB said Lagarde has not taken any decision 
regarding the end of her term.

Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskiy said he tasked his country’s 
negotiators to discuss the future of the Zaporizhzhia Nuclear Power Plant, 
which Russian forces seized early in the full-scale invasion,during peace talks 
in Geneva <https://bloom.bg/4tHg3ol>. He also said the third round of 
US-brokered talks with Russia would cover the monitoring of any agreed 
ceasefire, as well as the issue of Moscow’s territorial demands.

Warren Buffett’s Berkshire Hathaway slashed its stake in Amazon 
<https://bloom.bg/46Wy5Jv> by more than 75% in the fourth quarter, while also 
building a stake in the New York Times, 13F filings show in his last new bet as 
chief executive officer of the conglomerate. Berkshire acquired 5.1 million 
shares of the media publishing company in the three months through December, a 
stake worth $351.7 million at year-end. It took years forBuffett to embrace 
Amazon <https://bloom.bg/46Wy601>.

Japan plans to invest $36 billion in US oil, gas and critical minerals 
projects, the first tranche of its $550 billion commitmentunder the trade 
agreement <https://bloom.bg/4tyeDfU>. The most significant investment is a 
natural gas facility in Ohio, a massive project that Donald Trump described as 
“the largest in History.” Here’s our explainer about how America wasable to 
clinch such deals <https://bloom.bg/4tP56RZ> with countries including Japan and 
South Korea.

Apple is accelerating development of three new wearable devices as part of a 
shift toward AI-powered hardware, a category also being pursued by OpenAI and 
Meta. The companyis ramping up work <https://bloom.bg/3MtfZbh> on smart 
glasses, a pendant that can be pinned to a shirt or worn as a necklace, and 
AirPods with expanded AI capabilities, according to people with knowledge of 
the plans. All three devices are being built around the Siri digital assistant, 
which will rely on visual context to carry out actions. Read more about devices
Apple has been working on <https://bloom.bg/46Wy7RD>.
