**Colombia: Investor Sentiment Shifting**
Investors are beginning to eye what may be an inflection point in Colombia's policy direction, with coverage noting increased receptivity to market-friendly economic positioning from the country's leadership. The development is notable given the policy uncertainty that has weighed on Colombian assets in recent years. Whether this shift translates into durable improvements in the investment climate — or proves premature — remains to be seen, as structural fiscal and security challenges persist. No specific data releases were available for the period.
