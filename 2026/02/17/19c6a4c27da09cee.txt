The US and Iran are set to hold nuclear talks in Geneva, with Donald Trump 
saying Tehranwants to make a deal <https://bloom.bg/46Ey5he> and adding that he 
will be involved in the negotiations “indirectly.” Iran has beenconducting 
naval exercises <https://bloom.bg/4rUkKtq> in the region of a critical shipping 
corridor, Tasnim news agency reported. US-led talks toend the war in Ukraine 
are also scheduled <https://bloom.bg/4aATYzk> in the Swiss city.

BHP posted earnings at the upper end of analyst expectations, as a years-long 
effort toshore up copper production <https://bloom.bg/4c09nLW> combined with a 
historic metals rally to offset a lackluster period for its giant iron ore 
business. Copper, a metal favored for its vital role in a more electrified 
world, accounted for more than half of the group’s profit for the first time. 
The miner’s shares climbed to a record in Sydney.

 Deep Dive: Denmark’s Leader Looks to Election 


 <https://bloom.bg/4tXD7zF> Mette Frederiksen in January. Photographer: Simon 
Wohlfahrt/Bloomberg 
For Denmark’s prime minister, Trump’s Greenland grab now looks like a crisis 
too good to waste.

 * Public support has swung behind Mette Frederiksen on the back of her 
standoff with the US administration, leaving her weighing whether to call a 
snap election <https://bloom.bg/4tXD7zF> to capitalize on the momentum. The 
vote must be held by Oct. 31, but Frederiksen can summon Danes to the polls 
whenever she feels is most opportune. 
 * Frederiksen had looked to be in trouble prior to the conflict, her second 
term dragged down by domestic controversy. Rising costs of living and 
discontent over the government’s decision to scrap a public holiday were among 
the factors that drove her personal approval to a low in December. 
 * Two months on, Trump’s revival of  diplomatic tensions over the Arctic 
territory <https://bloom.bg/4rnyHjK> has shifted the political spotlight in 
Denmark to foreign policy and leadership. That’s playing to Frederiksen’s 
strengths, as she projects an image of a steady hand confronting an erratic 
superpower, with all of Europe at her back <https://bloom.bg/4rmrAIp>.
