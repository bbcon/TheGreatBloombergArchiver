DP World is offering тАЬwar riskтАЭ insurance for cargo owners trying to navigate 
Mideast trade routes disrupted by the US conflict with Iran.

The end-to-end coverage protects shipments across the supply chain тАФ ocean and 
air freight, inland haulage and storage. That way, it closes тАЬcritical gaps 
left by conventional insurance policies, which typically insure a single leg of 
the journey,тАЭ the Dubai-based port and logistics company said in astatement 
<https://www.dpworld.com/en/news/asset/aeadd49e-6e2c-4d76-8f88-898f7d878f27/2026_05_07dpworldlauncheswarriskinsuranceoffering>
.

DP World runs Jebel Ali port and the adjoining Jafza industrial area, which 
have remained open but largely isolated from seaborne trade during the closure 
of the Strait of Hormuz. The maritime facility and free-trade zone are vital 
economic hubs for goods trade in the Persian Gulf region.
