Welcome to our guide to the commodities driving the global economy. Today, 
Europe Oil Markets Team Leader Alaric Nightingale looks at how much crude is 
flowing from the Persian Gulf.

Whether Iran says the Strait of Hormuz is open or closed <>, the practical 
reality right now is that a large amount of Middle Eastern crude is making it 
out to world markets.

On Saturday alone, 17 million barrels flooded through the waterway, according 
to US Central Command. Such high flows would be close to prewar levels, 
although they canтАЩt be verified using the industryтАЩs digital tracking systems.

Visible flows, captured using signals from tankersтАЩ Automatic Identification 
System, are much lower, but still rising strongly. Over the past three days, 
such shipments through Hormuz have averaged 6.6 million barrels a day, 
according to data compiled by Bloomberg.

Such a discrepancy isnтАЩt surprising. Tracking signals have only given a 
partial picture since the war began as many nervous shipowners choosenot to 
broadcast 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-10/oil-tankers-go-dark-to-sneak-more-gulf-barrels-through-hormuz?cmpid=BBBXT062226_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260622&utm_content=5773>
 their locations while transiting the waterway.

Even so, the oil market is watching these figures closely because they will be 
indicative of the pace andscale of a revival 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-18/saudi-arabia-uae-oil-producers-gear-up-for-strait-of-hormuz-reopening?cmpid=BBBXT062226_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260622&utm_content=5773>
 in Middle East oil production, and ultimately the direction of crude prices.

Early on Saturday, there was a clear signal of confidence that one key route 
through Hormuz is safe.

The Joint Maritime Information Center, the liaison between military and 
merchant shippers, said vessels could go through a shipping lane that hugs 
OmanтАЩs coast with their locationsvisible to the world 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-20/ships-can-use-hormuz-southern-route-with-signals-on-jmic-says?cmpid=BBBXT062226_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260622&utm_content=5773>
.

While IranтАЩs media subsequently reported that the strait was closed, the 
countryтАЩs representatives later said that peace talks in Switzerlandwent well

Two laden oil tankers controlled by US-listed Nordic American Tankers Ltd.,

The duel of narratives between the US and Iran over Hormuz may continue, but
