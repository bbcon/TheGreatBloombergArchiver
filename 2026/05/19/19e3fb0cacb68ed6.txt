Xi told Trump that Putin might end up regretting his invasion of Ukraine, the 
Financial Times reported, citing sources.

Chart of the Day

Just half of 16-to-24-year-olds in the UK were in payrolled work at the end of 
last year, according to the Institute for Fiscal Studies analysis that 
underscores the scale of the country’s escalatingyouth jobless crisis 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-05-18/only-half-of-britain-s-young-people-have-paid-work-ifs-says?cmpid=BBD051926_politics&utm_campaign=bop&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260519&utm_content=4629>
. The proportion, 50.6%, has fallen from 54.9% at the end of 2022, a drop 
similar in scale to the pandemic and the 2008 financial crisis, the study of 
administrative data found.

And Finally

For generations, Dustin Morris and his family have owned farmland in Richland 
Parish, Louisiana, acreage whose value has always come from soil enriched by 
the silt of the Mississippi River. Here, in a poor corner of America, Meta is 
building one of theworld’s largest data center 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/features/2026-meta-facebook-ai-data-center-louisiana/?cmpid=BBD051926_politics&utm_campaign=bop&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260519&utm_content=4629>
. Dubbed Hyperion, it’s financed by one of the biggest private capital deals 
ever assembled, requires 10 new gas-fired turbines to power it — and is so 
secretive that the details were hashed out in an unusually sprawling set of 
private deals, leaving nearby residents like Morris to learn about it only 
after it was a fait accompli.
