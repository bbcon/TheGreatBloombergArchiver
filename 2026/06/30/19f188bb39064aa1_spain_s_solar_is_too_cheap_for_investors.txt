Over the past 15 years, Spain has been one of EuropeтАЩs fastest-growing

So much solar capacity was added last year that it flooded the grid, pushing 
prices deep below zero during peak times as producers cut rates to off-load 
excess power. Only six months into the year, the country has already surpassed 
its annual record for the number of hours when producers must pay users to take 
their electricity. The problem is happening across Europe, but itтАЩs most 
dramatic in Spain, where solar last year overtook wind as the largest source of
