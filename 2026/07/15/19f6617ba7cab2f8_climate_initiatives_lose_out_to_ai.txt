Burberry postponed its goal of cutting emissions to net zero by a decade to

About 58% of S&P 500 companies that have set goals to reduce their direct, or 
Scope 1, emissions have reported flat or rising emissions since 2021, according

generated by suppliers and customers and often account for the largest share of 
a companyтАЩs carbon footprint.

Looking ahead, only one-quarter of sustainability leaders surveyed said 
theyтАЩre confident their companies will meet environmental goals across most

тАЬIt wasnтАЩt political pressure that came out as the top reason to potentially

administrationтАЩs opposition to green policies. тАЬIt was capital allocation. 
Ultimately, thatтАЩs emerging as the real pressure point.тАЭ

The strain is notable in the technology sector where companies are racing to

Amazon.com Inc., Alphabet Inc.тАЩs Google and Microsoft Corp. spiked in 2025. 
Microsoft reported the biggest gain with emissions climbing 25%.
Cattle in Brazil.Photographer: Paulo Fridman/Bloomberg News
And itтАЩs more than just the tech industry thatтАЩs being affected. In May,
