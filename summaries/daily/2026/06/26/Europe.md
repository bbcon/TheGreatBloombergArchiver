**Macro: Southern Italy Rebalancing**
Campania, the region encompassing Naples, grew 0.9% in 2025, outpacing Lombardy's 0.7% — a notable reversal of the long-standing north-south divergence. The turnaround reflects a decade of EU Recovery Fund investment alongside private-sector commitments from Apple, Cisco, and Accenture. Morningstar DBRS notes that a more balanced Italian economy would strengthen fiscal resilience and reduce euro-area fragmentation risk. Separately, French Finance Minister Roland Lescure reaffirmed his commitment to deficit reduction, a signal that fiscal consolidation remains on the agenda despite political headwinds.

**Markets and Corporate**
Zalando shares fell as much as 11% in Frankfurt after Germany's financial regulator opened an investigation into the online retailer. Volkswagen may double planned staff reductions to 100,000 and shutter additional plants, according to Manager Magazin, as the company contends with weakness in China and intensifying domestic competition. The broader European equity market declined in tandem with the global tech selloff, with futures lower Friday morning.
