from odd jobs. “I’m slowly beginning to lose that hope,” he said.

and its contribution to the country’s economic output rivals that of the mining 
sector, but it is buckling under the pressure of rising costs and a flood of 
cheaper imports from India and China.
 * Companies, including giant manufacturers Toyota and Volkswagen, are 
clamoring for urgent policy interventions to help them compete.
 * In a rare alignment, unions and investors have joined forces to do the same.
