Even if these hurdles can be overcome, the supply-demand dynamics donтАЩt look 
favorable for Tehran.

About 68 million barrels of Iranian crude and condensate are already on the 
water тАФ most still available for purchase тАФ but the market is crowded.

Importers currently have a choice of spot crude cargoes from multiple sources, 
including Russia, the US and, increasingly, the Middle East, where exports from

Beijing is also in no rush to secure more supplies, since demand at home is

There are expectations that India, whose proximity to the Persian Gulf would 
allow it to take delivery within days, will snap up some Iranian cargoes, 
provided theyтАЩre discounted. South Korea could also be a buyer.

Interest from such players would see China lose some of its current dominance

the worldтАЩs biggest oil importer will likely maintain a strong hand.
