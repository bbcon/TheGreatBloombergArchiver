**Brazil Political Risk**
The Banco Master corruption allegations represent a potential vulnerability for the Lula administration, given Senator Wagner's proximity to the president. While the investigation remains at an early stage, the pattern of alleged corporate payments to political figures touches on longstanding concerns about the governance of Brazilian financial institutions. Markets will watch whether the probe expands in scope or implicates figures closer to the executive. Against the backdrop of a hawkish Fed and stronger dollar, Brazil and the broader LatAm region face an increasingly challenging external financing environment, with higher US rates reducing the relative appeal of EM local-currency assets that had benefited from the prior easing cycle.
