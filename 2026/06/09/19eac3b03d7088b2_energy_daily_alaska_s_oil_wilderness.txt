President Donald Trump has long touted the Arctic National Wildlife Refuge as 
the next great frontier for US oil production.

Yet the industryтАЩs biggest companies have balked at three opportunities since 
2021 to acquire drilling rights for ANWR. The political realities that drove 
those decisions donтАЩt seem to have changed.

That was evident on Friday, when not a single oil major participated in an

<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-06-05/trump-s-alaska-oil-and-gas-lease-auction-draws-just-two-bidders?cmpid=BBBXT060926_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260609&utm_content=5334>
: Anchorage-based Hex Energy and the Alaska Industrial Development and Export

Oil companiesтАЩ lack of interest in ANWR is in stark contrast to their

LastтАЩs week auction doesnтАЩt spell the end of efforts to open up ANWR, as it
