Some highlights from the report:

 * Chip exports surged 183%, driving overall shipments amid strong investment 
in AI and data centers.
 * Shipments of exports of computer peripherals surged 399%.
 * Oil products also contributed to gains, rising 48%, with energy exports 
partly lifted by higher crude prices.
 * Autos and auto parts both declined.
 * By destination, exports to China jumped 71%, while shipments to the US 
increased 52%.
