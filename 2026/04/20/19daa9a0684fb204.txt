But with the US refusing to lift its own blockade, the Iranians reversed their

One Indian tanker 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-19/gunfire-u-turns-and-threats-mark-a-chaotic-weekend-in-hormuz?cmpid=BBBXT042026_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260420>

given clearance to transit, but was instead getting shot at.

Other vessels attempting to exit then U-turned en masse.

Not to be outdone, the US Navy fired upon and boarded an Iranian-flagged cargo 
ship in the Gulf of Oman, the first seizure of a vessel since it started its 
blockade.
