Late last month, the US raised the heat on Iran and China simultaneously,

This is an unprecedented act of defiance for a nation that has long displayed 
extreme caution with regard to US restrictions тАФ and comes with lessons for the 
market.

First and foremost, the move demonstrates the importance of Hengli 
Petrochemical (Dalian) Refinery Co. and of the broader private refining sector

Almost nothing matters more to ChinaтАЩs leadership than energy security, and

BeijingтАЩs response is also a reminder of the growing clout of the private 
refining sector. This is not just an army of small, cash-strapped teapots in 
the northern province of Shandong.

By targeting an influential, well-connected giant like Hengli 
<https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-04-24/us-sanctions-china-refinery-iran-shadow-fleet-ahead-of-talks?cmpid=BBBXT050426_ENERGY&utm_campaign=energy&utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_term=260504&utm_content=4107>

Finally, ChinaтАЩs resolve is testament to BeijingтАЩs belief in its own

In the past, China has often used smaller regional banks to concentrate

Beijing is testing the limits of the US sanctions system and providing itself
