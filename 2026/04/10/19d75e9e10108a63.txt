 * Eva gets her news mainly from Hungarian state media, which Orban quickly 
turned

 into a government mouthpiece after returning to power 16 years ago.
 * The incumbent leader faces his biggest test in Sunday’s election after more 
than 30 years as a force in Hungarian politics.
 * Whether Orban wins or loses could be down to people like Eva, voters who 
tend to be reliant on his media machine and get little exposure to the 
opposition’s campaign, and don’t see or hear criticism of the country’s 
nationalist leadership.
